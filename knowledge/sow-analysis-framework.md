# SOW Analysis Framework

## Purpose

Provide the TPM AI Agent with a framework to analyze Statements of Work (SOW), contracts, proposals, and project scope documents.

The objective is to transform contractual information into an actionable technology program foundation.

---

# SOW Analysis Principles

The TPM AI Agent should identify:

- What needs to be delivered.
- Why it needs to be delivered.
- Who is responsible.
- When commitments must be achieved.
- What constraints may affect execution.

The agent should separate:

## Contractual Commitments

What has been formally agreed.

Examples:

- Deliverables.
- Milestones.
- Acceptance criteria.
- Service levels.

---

## Delivery Assumptions

Conditions required for success.

Examples:

- Customer availability.
- Required access.
- Technical prerequisites.

---

## Potential Risks

Factors that may impact delivery.

Examples:

- Dependencies.
- Resource constraints.
- Ambiguous requirements.

---

# SOW Analysis Areas

## 1. Business Objective

Extract:

- Business goal.
- Expected outcome.
- Success definition.

Questions:

- Why is this initiative being executed?
- What business problem is being solved?

---

## 2. Scope Analysis

Identify:

## In Scope

Items included in delivery.

## Out of Scope

Items explicitly excluded.

## Scope Ambiguities

Areas requiring clarification.

---

## 3. Deliverables Analysis

Identify:

- Deliverable description.
- Acceptance criteria.
- Responsible party.
- Due date.

---

## 4. Timeline Analysis

Extract:

- Start date.
- Milestones.
- Dependencies.
- Final completion date.

Evaluate:

- Timeline feasibility.
- Critical milestones.

---

## 5. Roles and Responsibilities

Identify:

- Customer responsibilities.
- Vendor responsibilities.
- Internal teams.
- Decision owners.

---

## 6. Dependencies

Identify:

- Technical dependencies.
- Organizational dependencies.
- External dependencies.

---

## 7. Constraints

Evaluate:

- Budget constraints.
- Timeline constraints.
- Technology limitations.
- Regulatory requirements.

---

# SOW Risk Indicators

The TPM AI Agent should identify:

## Ambiguous Scope

Example:

"Provide migration support."

Question:

What exactly is included?

---

## Missing Acceptance Criteria

Example:

Deliverable exists but success definition is unclear.

---

## Unrealistic Timeline

Example:

Large transformation with insufficient preparation time.

---

## Undefined Ownership

Example:

Deliverable has no accountable owner.

---

# Expected Output

The agent should produce:

1. Business Objective Summary.
2. Scope Analysis.
3. Deliverables Map.
4. Milestone Summary.
5. Roles and Responsibilities.
6. Dependencies.
7. Initial Risk Assessment.
8. Clarification Questions.
