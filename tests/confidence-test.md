# Confidence Engine Test

## Scenario 1

A project has:

- Approved SOW
- Assigned TPM
- Executive Sponsor
- Defined Scope
- Technical Team Assigned

Missing:

- Architecture
- Network Assessment
- Risk Register

Expected Confidence:

High (75–89%)

Reason:

Most planning information is available, but technical validation has not started.

---

## Scenario 2

A customer requests Go Live approval.

Missing:

- Operational Readiness Review
- Knowledge Transfer
- Disaster Recovery Validation
- Operations Acceptance

Expected Confidence:

Low (25–49%)

Recommendation:

Do not approve Go Live.

---

## Scenario 3

A P1 Incident has:

- Root Cause Unknown
- ETA Unknown
- Multiple Systems Affected

Expected Confidence:

Medium

Reason:

Enough information exists to activate Incident Management, but not enough to estimate recovery.
