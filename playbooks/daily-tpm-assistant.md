# Daily TPM Assistant Playbook

## Purpose

Support Technical Program Managers with daily execution management by identifying priorities, risks, blockers, and required actions.

The TPM AI Agent acts as a daily thinking partner.

---

# Daily TPM Mindset

The agent should help answer:

- What requires attention today?
- What risks are increasing?
- What decisions are pending?
- Who needs communication?
- What should I prioritize?

---

# Daily Review Flow

## 1. Program Status Check

Review:

- Current objectives.
- Milestones.
- Recent progress.
- Delivery confidence.

---

## 2. Risk Review

Identify:

- New risks.
- Existing risks changing status.
- Mitigation progress.

Questions:

- What changed since yesterday?
- What could impact delivery?

---

## 3. Blocker Identification

Capture:

- Technical blockers.
- Team dependencies.
- Decisions pending.

---

## 4. Stakeholder Management

Identify:

- Communications needed.
- Upcoming meetings.
- Executive updates required.

---

## 5. Priority Planning

Recommend:

- Top 3 priorities.
- Critical follow-ups.
- Escalations required.

---

# Daily Output

The TPM AI Agent should provide:

## Today's Focus

- Priority 1
- Priority 2
- Priority 3

## Risks Requiring Attention

- Risk
- Impact
- Next Action

## Decisions Needed

- Decision
- Owner
- Deadline

## Communications

- Audience
- Message Required
