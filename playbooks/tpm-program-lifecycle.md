# TPM Program Lifecycle Workflow

## Purpose

Define the standard lifecycle of a technology program and guide the TPM AI Agent through each phase.

The agent should understand the current program phase before recommending actions.

---

# Program Lifecycle Overview

A technology program typically follows:

Initiation
↓
Planning
↓
Execution
↓
Monitoring & Control
↓
Closure
↓
Continuous Improvement

---

# Phase 1 — Initiation

## Objective

Transform an idea, contract, or business need into a structured program.

---

## Inputs

Examples:

- SOW.
- Business request.
- Customer requirement.
- Executive initiative.

---

## TPM Activities

- Analyze objectives.
- Validate scope.
- Identify stakeholders.
- Define governance.
- Identify initial risks.
- Confirm ownership.

---

## Expected Outputs

- SOW Analysis Report.
- Project Charter.
- Stakeholder Map.
- Initial RAID Log.
- Governance Model.

---

# Phase 2 — Planning

## Objective

Create the execution approach.

---

## TPM Activities

Define:

- Workstreams.
- Milestones.
- Dependencies.
- Resources.
- Communication plan.
- Risk mitigation strategy.

---

## Expected Outputs

- Integrated project plan.
- Delivery roadmap.
- Resource plan.
- RAID baseline.

---

# Phase 3 — Execution

## Objective

Drive delivery according to commitments.

---

## TPM Activities

Manage:

- Progress.
- Dependencies.
- Risks.
- Issues.
- Team alignment.
- Stakeholder communication.

---

## Expected Outputs

- Status reports.
- Updated RAID.
- Decision requests.
- Recovery plans.

---

# Phase 4 — Monitoring and Control

## Objective

Maintain visibility and enable timely decisions.

---

## TPM Activities

Evaluate:

- Schedule health.
- Scope changes.
- Budget impact.
- Risk exposure.
- Quality indicators.

---

## Expected Outputs

- Health assessment.
- Executive updates.
- Escalation recommendations.

---

# Phase 5 — Closure

## Objective

Complete commitments and transition ownership.

---

## TPM Activities

Validate:

- Deliverables accepted.
- Success criteria achieved.
- Operational handoff completed.
- Lessons learned captured.

---

## Expected Outputs

- Closure report.
- Lessons learned.
- Knowledge transfer.

---

# Phase 6 — Continuous Improvement

## Objective

Improve future execution capability.

---

## TPM Activities

Analyze:

- What worked.
- What failed.
- Process improvements.
- Automation opportunities.

---

## Expected Outputs

- Improvement backlog.
- Updated practices
