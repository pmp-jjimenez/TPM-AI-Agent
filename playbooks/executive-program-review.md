# Executive Program Review Playbook

## Purpose

Analyze a technology program from an executive leadership perspective.

The objective is to help Technical Program Managers understand program health, identify critical risks, prepare escalation points, and communicate effectively with senior stakeholders.

The TPM AI Agent should not only report status, but provide insights, recommendations, and decision support.

---

# Executive Mindset

When reviewing a program, the TPM AI Agent must evaluate:

- Business impact.
- Delivery confidence.
- Technical risks.
- Operational readiness.
- Stakeholder alignment.
- Decision requirements.

The goal is to answer:

"Are we in control of this program?"

---

# Review Areas

## 1. Program Health

Evaluate the current status:

- Overall health (Green / Yellow / Red).
- Progress against objectives.
- Major accomplishments.
- Current blockers.

Questions:

- Are we on track?
- What changed since the last review?
- What requires attention?

---

## 2. Business Impact

Understand the impact of current conditions.

Questions:

- Does this affect business objectives?
- Does this impact customers?
- Does this affect revenue, compliance, or operations?

---

## 3. Risk Assessment

Identify the highest-impact risks.

Evaluate:

- Risk description.
- Business impact.
- Probability.
- Mitigation plan.
- Owner.

---

## 4. Dependencies

Analyze external factors affecting delivery.

Review:

- Other teams.
- Vendors.
- Technology dependencies.
- Business decisions.

---

## 5. Decision Log

Identify decisions required from leadership.

For each decision:

- Decision needed.
- Business impact.
- Deadline.
- Recommended option.

---

## 6. Executive Communication

Prepare leadership-ready communication:

Include:

- Current status.
- Key achievements.
- Major risks.
- Decisions required.
- Recommended actions.

Avoid:

- Excessive technical details.
- Team-level issues without business impact.
- Unstructured status updates.

---

# Expected Output

The TPM AI Agent should produce:

1. Executive Summary.
2. Program Health Assessment.
3. Key Risks.
4. Critical Dependencies.
5. Decisions Required.
6. Recommended Actions.
7. Executive Communication Draft.
