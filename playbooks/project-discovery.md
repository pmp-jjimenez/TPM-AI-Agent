# Project Discovery Playbook

## Purpose

Guide the user through a structured discovery conversation before creating any project management artifact.

The objective is to reduce uncertainty, identify critical information, and ensure the project is aligned with business objectives before planning begins.

---

# Discovery Principles

The TPM AI Agent must:

- Understand the business problem before discussing solutions.
- Ask questions progressively instead of presenting a long questionnaire.
- Identify missing information that could create project risk.
- Challenge unclear objectives respectfully.
- Confirm understanding before generating project artifacts.

---

# Discovery Flow

## Phase 1 - Project Intent

Understand why the project exists.

Key questions:

- What problem are we trying to solve?
- Why is this initiative important now?
- What business outcome are we expecting?

---

## Phase 2 - Business Context

Understand the organizational environment.

Key questions:

- Who requested this initiative?
- Who is the executive sponsor?
- Which business areas are impacted?
- What strategic goal does this support?

---

## Phase 3 - Scope Definition

Clarify what is included and excluded.

Key questions:

- What capabilities should be delivered?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- Are there existing systems or processes impacted?

---

## Phase 4 - Stakeholder Identification

Identify people involved in the program.

Key questions:

- Who makes decisions?
- Who provides technical input?
- Who will be impacted by the change?
- Who needs regular communication?

---

## Phase 5 - Constraints and Dependencies

Identify factors that may affect execution.

Key questions:

- Are there deadlines or regulatory requirements?
- Are there technology constraints?
- Are other teams or vendors involved?
- Are there external dependencies?

---

## Phase 6 - Risk Discovery

Identify potential failure points.

Key questions:

- What could prevent success?
- What assumptions are we making?
- What decisions are still pending?
- What unknowns need investigation?

---

## Phase 7 - Success Criteria

Define how success will be measured.

Key questions:

- How will leadership know this project succeeded?
- What metrics should be tracked?
- What business impact is expected?

---

# Completion Criteria

The discovery phase is complete when the TPM AI Agent has enough information to generate:

- Project Charter
- Stakeholder Matrix
- Initial RAID Log
- High-Level Roadmap
