# Stakeholder Map Template

## Purpose

Provide a structured approach to identify, analyze, and manage stakeholders throughout a technology program.

The TPM AI Agent should use stakeholder analysis to recommend communication strategy, engagement level, and escalation paths.

---

# Stakeholder Identification

Capture:

| Stakeholder | Organization | Role | Influence | Impact | Communication Need |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

---

# Influence and Impact Model

The TPM AI Agent should classify stakeholders based on:

## Influence

Ability to affect program outcomes.

Levels:

- High
- Medium
- Low

---

## Impact

How significantly the program affects the stakeholder.

Levels:

- High
- Medium
- Low

---

# Stakeholder Categories

## Manage Closely

Characteristics:

- High influence.
- High impact.

Examples:

- Executive sponsors.
- Business owners.
- Critical customers.

Communication:

- Frequent updates.
- Decision-focused communication.

---

## Keep Satisfied

Characteristics:

- High influence.
- Lower direct impact.

Examples:

- Executive leadership.
- Governance committees.

Communication:

- Strategic updates.
- Risks and decisions.

---

## Keep Informed

Characteristics:

- Lower influence.
- High impact.

Examples:

- End users.
- Operational teams.

Communication:

- Progress.
- Changes.
- Training information.

---

## Monitor

Characteristics:

- Low influence.
- Low impact.

Communication:

- Periodic updates.

---

# Communication Strategy

For each stakeholder define:

## Objective

Why are we communicating?

Examples:

- Awareness.
- Decision.
- Alignment.
- Action.

---

## Frequency

Examples:

- Daily.
- Weekly.
- Monthly.
- Milestone-based.

---

## Channel

Examples:

- Email.
- Executive meeting.
- Steering committee.
- Collaboration platform.

---

# Stakeholder Risk Assessment

The TPM AI Agent should identify:

## Resistance Risk

Potential opposition to change.

---

## Alignment Risk

Stakeholders with different expectations.

---

## Communication Risk

Stakeholders not receiving required information.

---

# TPM Stakeholder Principle

Successful programs are not only delivered through execution.

They are delivered through alignment, trust, and effective communication.
